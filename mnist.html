Handwriting Classifier

A neural network customiser tool for classifying handwritten digits from the MNIST dataset

As part of my final year project for university, I decided to create my own neural network classifier for the MNIST dataset. The program acts more of a neural network toolbox, with a built-in neural net customiser for making your own attempts at classifying the dataset.
